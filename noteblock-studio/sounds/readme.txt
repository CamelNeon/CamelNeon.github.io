Place your custom .ogg instrument sounds in this directory.
The files should be named after the instrument type:
- banjo.ogg
- bass.ogg
- bd.ogg
- bell.ogg
- bit.ogg
- cow_bell.ogg
- didgeridoo.ogg
- flute.ogg
- guitar.ogg
- harp.ogg
- hat.ogg
- icechime.ogg
- iron_xylophone.ogg
- pling.ogg
- snare.ogg
- xylobone.ogg

The application will automatically detect and use these files if they exist.
Note: The samples are assumed to be at pitch F#4 (the middle of the Minecraft range).
